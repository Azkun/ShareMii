    About ShareMii

    ShareMii operates entirely in your browser. Drop your save files and modify your Miis directly!

    To use this tool:

    • Dump your save using Checkpoint or JKSV on your Switch.
    • Back up the dumped folder before doing anything.
    • Upload the save folder or its .zip here.
    • Browse your Miis, and Export any Mii as a .ltd file.
    • Import a .ltd file to replace a Mii's appearance and personality.
    • Click Export as .ZIP, then restore the modified save with Checkpoint!

    Tips:

    • Modifying Draft Mii will only change its appearance.
    • Modifying an existing mii will change its appearance and personality, but not its items or relationships.
